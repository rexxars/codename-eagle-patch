===========================================================================
================ Unofficial Codename Eagle v1.43 Patch ===================
===========================================================================

The unofficial version 1.43 of Codename Eagle is, in no way, endorsed or
supported by DICE or Talonsoft. It was created by Dafoosa to lift several
of the engine's limits and fix long-standing annoyances, and it became the
standard version for online play. It applies on top of the official v1.41
update and the unofficial v1.42 SEB fix (see readme141.txt/readme142.txt).

Changes in v1.43:

- Removes the 2000 limit on the "viewdist" console command
- Removes the 100-500 limit on the "fov" console command
- Removes the zoom-out and zoom-in limits in the F9 free camera view
- Lengthens the vehicle popup distance so vehicles appear sooner
- Fixes CE hanging intermittently upon exiting the torpedo boat
- Fixes font scaling at higher resolutions
- Shows teammates with a "T" in the tab map
- Fixes a bug in the scripting function "REFGetPlayerInx"

Credits: Dafoosa

More on the full patch history: https://codenameeagle.net/patches/
